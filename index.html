Hello, I am Chih Hsiao.

With a background in fine art and human-computer interaction (HCI), Chih has over 20 years of experience designing software applications across web, television, social network, mobile, and desktop operating systems.

Since the early 2000s, he has served in various design leadership roles at Apple, Yahoo, and Electronic Arts as his career expands over several industries such as advertising, gaming, search, and cloud technologies. He is currently leading the UX and front-end development teams at a SaaS company, building products that focus on AI-powered search technologies and big data analytics.
